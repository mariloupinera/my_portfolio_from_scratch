My Projects

Uplift (Mobile First)

During the Pandemic times, getting an appointment for home repairs via phonecall is quite impossible, or companies send their workers without reassuring if they can deliver a good quality service or not, because of this, we tend to repeat the process until their job well done. The problem is, we consume a lot of time, money, and effort fixing one problem. This app was built to help people and make Life Easier, less money, time and worry. Simple problem to a simple solution. By using this app, everyone can post a favour or help someone around your Neighborhood. Determining if a worker is qualified or capable through ratings and recommendations from his past works by checking His profile page. Both Users can Chat privately discussing about their agreements, and finally earning points after a job well done to access a paid job search in the website. Unfortunately the website is not ready.

Flying Wheels (Airbnb-like Website)

The Netherlands is well-known for their bicycle paths and it's people riding bicycles not only as a sport but as one of their mode of transportation. Tourists often rent a bike to experience this one in a lifetime opportunity, that is what this website made for. Users can search and book a bike nearby their preferred location with the price, date and time, or a User can post his bike for rent.
